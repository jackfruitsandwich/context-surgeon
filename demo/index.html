03 / THE INSTRUMENTS

Three incisions

A scalpel, not a sledgehammer. The agent decides what to keep, what to compress, and what can come back — based on what it's working on right now.

reversible
⌦

Evict ·removeᐧ

Cut content out of context, leave a placeholder. The original is held in a shadow store — nothing is truly lost.

lossy
⤳

Replace ·sutureᐧ

Swap a 15KB tool dump for a one-line summary the agent writes itself. Keep the meaning, shed the weight.

recall
⟲

Restore ·reviveᐧ

Bring evicted content back the moment it matters again. The scalpel works both ways.
